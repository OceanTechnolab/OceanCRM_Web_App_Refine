## ğŸ“ What Are Lead Generation Forms?

Lead Generation forms are Facebook's built-in forms that allow people to express interest in your business without leaving Facebook. When someone clicks your ad or link, a pre-filled form pops up making it super easy for them to submit their information.

### Why Use Them?

- âœ… **Pre-filled data** - Facebook auto-fills name, email, phone from user's profile
- âœ… **Mobile-optimized** - Works perfectly on phones
- âœ… **No landing page needed** - Form is hosted by Facebook
- âœ… **Higher conversion** - Less friction = more leads
- âœ… **Direct CRM integration** - That's what you're building!

## ğŸ“‹ Minimum Requirements for a Form

To create a functional lead form, you need:

1. **A Facebook Page** (not personal profile)

2. **Privacy Policy URL**
